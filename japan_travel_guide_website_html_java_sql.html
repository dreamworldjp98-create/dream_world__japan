Travel Guide Overview

This guide helps you explore Japan with ease including destinations, best time, and budget tips.

Top Destinations

  • Tokyo - Modern city life
  • Kyoto - Temples & culture
  • Osaka - Food & nightlife
  • Hokkaido - Snow & nature

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March-May) for cherry blossoms, Autumn (Sept-Nov) for cool weather.

Accommodation

Budget: Hostels, Airbnb | Luxury: Hotels via Booking.com

Important Tips

  • Use metro cards (Suica/Pasmo)
  • Carry cash
  • Respect local culture
  • Use Google Maps for navigation
