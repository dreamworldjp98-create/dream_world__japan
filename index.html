Tokyo 🏙️

Japan ki capital city – modern life, shopping, technology.

  • Best Time: March–May 🌸
  • Budget: ₹6000–₹15000/day
  • Top Places: Shibuya Crossing, Tokyo Tower

Kyoto ⛩️

Traditional Japan – temples aur culture.

  • Best Time: Oct–Nov 🍁
  • Budget: ₹5000–₹12000/day
  • Top Places: Fushimi Inari Shrine, Gion

Osaka 🍜

Food capital of Japan – street food & nightlife.

  • Best Time: March–May
  • Budget: ₹5000–₹13000/day
  • Top Places: Dotonbori, Osaka Castle

Hokkaido ❄️

Snow, mountains & nature lovers ke liye perfect.

  • Best Time: Dec–Feb (Snow)
  • Budget: ₹6000–₹14000/day
  • Top Places: Sapporo, Niseko
