✈️ Arrival & Airports

Haneda, Narita, and Kansai are the main hubs. Use Visit Japan Web for digital immigration to save time.

💴 Budget System

Japan is accessible for every wallet:

  • Budget: $50–$75 / day
  • Mid-Range: $100–$180 / day
  • Luxury: $250+ / day

Use 7-Eleven ATMs for the best currency exchange rates.

🚆 Transport

Get a Suica or Pasmo card for local travel. The Shinkansen is the fastest way between cities.

🏙️ Top Cities

  • Tokyo – Modern Life & Neon Lights
  • Kyoto – Ancient Temples & Tradition
  • Osaka – The World's Kitchen
  • Nara – Famous Deer Park Experience

🍜 Food Guide

Try Sushi, Ramen, and Takoyaki. Convenience stores (Conbini) offer high-quality, affordable meals.

🎥 Creator Secrets

For viral travel content:

  • Rainy shots = Cinematic aesthetic 🌧️
  • Neon Night = Best for high-energy Reels
  • Early Morning = Capture empty streets
