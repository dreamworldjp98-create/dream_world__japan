Home Guide Plan

✈️ Arrival & Airports

Haneda, Narita, Kansai main airports. Use Visit Japan Web for fast immigration.

💴 Budget System

Budget ₹4k–6k/day | Mid ₹8k–15k | Luxury ₹20k+

Use 7-Eleven ATMs. Cash still important.

🚆 Transport

Use Suica / Pasmo. Shinkansen fastest. JR Pass not always worth.

🏨 Stay

Capsule, business hotel, ryokan. Always stay near station.

🍜 Food Guide

Sushi, ramen, takoyaki. Convenience store food = cheap & good.

🏙️ Cities

  • Tokyo – modern life
  • Kyoto – temples
  • Osaka – food
  • Nara – deer park
  • Hokkaido – snow

💡 Pro Tips

  • No tipping ❌
  • Silence in trains
  • Carry cash
  • Use Google Maps
