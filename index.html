Tokyo 🏙️ Popular

Modern Japan ka heart – shopping, anime, technology.

  • Best Time: March–May 🌸
  • Budget: ₹6000–₹15000/day
  • Top Places: Shibuya Crossing, Akihabara, Tokyo Tower

Kyoto ⛩️

Traditional Japan – temples aur rich culture.

  • Best Time: October–November 🍁
  • Budget: ₹5000–₹12000/day
  • Top Places: Fushimi Inari Shrine, Gion District

Osaka 🍜

Food lover's paradise – amazing street food & nightlife.

  • Best Time: March–May
  • Budget: ₹5000–₹13000/day
  • Top Places: Dotonbori, Osaka Castle

Hokkaido ❄️

Snow & nature lovers ke liye perfect destination.

  • Best Time: December–February (Snow)
  • Budget: ₹6000–₹14000/day
  • Top Places: Sapporo, Niseko Ski Resorts

Hiroshima 🌸

Peace memorial, history, and beautiful coastal views.

  • Budget: ₹5000–₹11000/day
  • Top Places: Peace Memorial Park, Miyajima Shrine

💡 Pro Travel Tips

  • JR Pass: Buy before you travel to save on bullet trains 🚄
  • Cash is King: Humesha cash carry karo, many places don't take cards 💴
  • Navigation: Google Maps is your best friend for train schedules.
  • Manners: Always respect local culture and keep quiet on trains 🙏

💸 Cheap Travel Tricks

  • Food: Convenience store (7-Eleven/Lawson) bento boxes bahut saste aur tasty hote hain 🍱
  • Transport: Long distance ke liye Night Bus use karo, hotel cost bachegi 🚌
  • Stay: Hostels ya capsule hotels use karo instead of luxury hotels 🛏️
  • Timing: Off-season travel karo for cheaper flights ✈️
